About Billie

Billie Balloo is her name, and trust me baby, you could never wear it out. She's fly as a bird in the sky, went up lookin for her mind, got caught up in the hype, and never came down. Her smooth voice will reach out and carress your inner flirtations, and her paintings will envigorate your soul like the molecules in a Life Saver when it gets chewed. Buzzed and excited.

If you don't like blues, get out of Billie's house.She loves like a bear hug, fights like a grizzly, and scratches her back with the blues.
